## "The Thursday Incident" (2015)

### Meeting Context
- Quarterly strategic review Board meeting
- Full Board attendance including The Board Director in medically-induced coma
- Fred scheduled to present ReAnimus Initiative progress update
- Larry prepared with comprehensive opposition documentation
- Tensions high following weeks of private disagreement
- Pneumonia Pete present as operations representative
- Meeting held in secure conference room with recording disabled

### Incident Sequence
1. Fred presents misleading data on ReAnimus achievements
2. Larry challenges accuracy of safety and ethics claims
3. Fred dismisses concerns as "bureaucratic handwringing"
4. Larry presents documented protocol violations
5. Fred becomes increasingly agitated during exchange
6. Heated argument escalates with personal accusations
7. Critical moment: Fred uses "Francis Underpantsis" trigger during presentation
8. Larry visibly affected by childhood nickname deployment
9. Board witnesses Larry's emotional vulnerability for first time
10. Meeting descends into unprecedented personal conflict

### "Francis Underpantsis" Deployment
- Fred strategically uses childhood trigger during professional presentation
- Public humiliation in front of Board and executive leadership
- Calculated to undermine Larry's professional standing
- Designed to distract from substantive ReAnimus concerns
- Deliberately targeting known emotional vulnerability
- Unprecedented breach of professional decorum
- Fundamental violation of unspoken professional boundaries

### Immediate Aftermath
- Brief but visible emotional reaction from Larry
- Rapid recovery and composed response surprising observers
- Meeting temporarily adjourned for "technical issues"
- Emergency executive session called without Fred and Larry
- The Board's exceptional intervention in operational conflict
- Development of short-term containment strategy
- Implementation of forced cooling-off period
