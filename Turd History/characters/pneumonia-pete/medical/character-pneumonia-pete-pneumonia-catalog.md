This document provides a comprehensive catalog of all 92 documented pneumonia cases experienced by Peter "Pneumonia Pete" Sebastian, including essential details about each case, their personified identities, and their significance within his medical history.

## Pattern Analysis

### Timeline Distribution
- Childhood (1-20): 1974-1984 - One case every ~6 months
- Early Adulthood (21-40): 1985-1994 - One case every ~6 months
- Professional Period (41-70): 1995-2009 - One case every ~5 months
- Recent Period (71-92): 2010-Present - One case every ~6 months

### Duration Evolution
- Childhood (1-20): Average duration 39.6 days
- Early Adulthood (21-40): Average duration 34.2 days
- Professional Period (41-70): Average duration 21.7 days
- Recent Period (71-92): Average duration 14.3 days

### Severity Progression
- Clinical death incidents: 27 total
  - Childhood (1-20): 4 incidents
  - Early Adulthood (21-40): 8 incidents
  - Professional Period (41-70): 9 incidents
  - Recent Period (71-92): 6 incidents
- Hospitalization requirements: Decreased from 100% (cases 1-30) to 45% (cases 71-92)
- Ventilator dependency: Decreased from average 18 days (cases 1-20) to 3 days (cases 71-92)
