## Context
This document details Peter "Pneumonia Pete" Sebastian's early career at Turd Bird Industries, covering his transition from experimental test subject to corporate executive. This period established the foundation for his later ascension to CEO and his complex relationship with Fred Turd.

## Initial Engagement (2000)

### The Experimental Trial

#### Recruitment Circumstances
- Initially approached as potential test subject for experimental compound XR-472
- Volunteered for trial after 12 previous subjects had declined participation
- Identified through medical research database as "extreme outlier"
- Specifically requested by Fred Turd after reading medical case history
- Agreed to participate despite 97% estimated risk of severe complications
- Negotiated unprecedented access to trial data as participation condition

#### The XR-472 Trial Experience
- Compound designed to enhance cellular regeneration under extreme stress
- Previous animal testing showed promise but severe side effects
- Human trials had been halted after dangerous reactions in first three subjects
- Pete tolerated 20x the maximum previously administered dose
- Experienced "moderate discomfort" where others had suffered organ failure
- Provided detailed symptom reporting during 72-hour observation period
- Demonstrated unusual physiological response pattern noted by researchers

#### Post-Trial Observations
- Pete's body metabolized compound in unprecedented manner
- Blood work showed unique adaptation pattern of increasing tolerance
- Requested and received permission to self-administer follow-up doses
- Documented previously unseen cellular regeneration in damaged lung tissue
- Approached research team with suggested compound modifications
- Presented detailed self-experimentation protocol for approval

### Transition to Consultant

#### Fred Turd's Recognition
- Fred personally reviewed trial results with research team
- Identified Pete as "the perfect impossible test case"
- Recognized potential value beyond single trial participation
- Appreciated Pete's methodical self-documentation approach
- Noted unusual combination of medical knowledge and personal experience
- Saw opportunity to accelerate testing timelines using Pete's tolerance

#### Initial Consulting Arrangement
- Offered position as "Special Medical Consultant"
- Engaged for product safety assessment at executive level
- Provided with direct reporting line to Fred Turd
- Given unprecedented access to experimental development pipeline
- Established as final approval authority for human testing protocols
- Assigned dedicated laboratory space for personal monitoring

#### Early Contributions
- Developed streamlined testing protocol for high-risk compounds
- Created "Sebastian Safety Matrix" for risk assessment
- Personally tested 17 compounds in first three months
- Established comprehensive documentation methodology
- Implemented ethical framework for extreme product testing
- Reduced product development timeline by 63% through improved protocols
