- Process: Extended silence while consciousness synchronization occurs
- Duration: Precisely 19.85 minutes (reference to founding year)
- Physical Components: Specific breathing patterns and positioning
- Participation: Required for all members capable of consciousness
- Effect: Creates shared cognitive workspace for complex deliberation
- Location Restriction: Only possible in The Void

#### "The Binding"

- Process: Specialized synchronized movement pattern among members
- Duration: Variable based on decision significance (3-12 minutes)

- Duration: Exactly 7 minutes

- Participation: Human members primarily with AI verification

- Location Restriction: Only possible in facilities with reality anchors

- Process: Specialized consciousness synchronization protocol
- Duration: 13 minutes
- Physical Components: Specific arrangement with equidistant spacing
- Participation: All members regardless of form

- Location Restriction: Only possible in specially equipped facilities
