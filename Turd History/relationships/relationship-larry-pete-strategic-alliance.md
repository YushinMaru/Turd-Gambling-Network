## Context
This document details the strategic alliance between Lawrence "Larry" Francis Bird and Peter "Pneumonia Pete" Sebastian that evolved from initial professional skepticism into one of Turd Bird Industries' most consequential partnerships. Their alliance, founded on shared concerns about Fred Turd's increasingly erratic behavior, fundamentally altered corporate governance dynamics and continues to influence company direction despite Larry's mysterious departure.
