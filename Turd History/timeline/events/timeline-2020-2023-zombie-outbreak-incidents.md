## Context

This document chronicles the significant zombie containment breaches and outbreak incidents resulting from the ReAnimus Initiative between 2020-2023, including the Headquarters Sub-Level Incident, the Moore Building Event, the Arctic Facility Breach, and the Pneumonia Pete Immunity Discovery, as well as the corporate containment protocols, immunity developments, and public knowledge suppression strategies implemented in response.

## Classification System and Terminology

### Subject Classification
- **Class A**: Minimal reanimation with no consciousness indicators
- **Class B**: Basic reanimation with reflex responses
- **Class C**: Partial consciousness with limited cognitive function
- **Class D**: Significant consciousness with memory fragments
- **Class E**: Advanced reanimation with substantial consciousness preservation

### Zombie Classification
- **Type 1**: Slow-moving with minimal cognitive function (derived from Class A/B)
- **Type 2**: Standard mobility with rudimentary hunting instincts (derived from Class C)
- **Type 3**: Enhanced mobility with problem-solving capability (derived from Class C/D)
- **Type 4**: Specialized adaptation zombies with unique characteristics (environmental variants)
- **Type 5**: "Alpha" zombie with coordination capabilities and rudimentary command functions

### Containment Categories
- **Code Green**: Contained breach with no risk of exposure beyond laboratory
- **Code Yellow**: Breach within secured facility area requiring specialized response
- **Code Orange**: Facility-wide breach with intact exterior containment
- **Code Red**: Exterior containment breach with limited external exposure
- **Code Black**: Catastrophic containment failure with significant external exposure

## Early Containment Challenges (2018-2019)

### First Observed Zombie Characteristics
- Initial zombie-like behaviors observed in Subject RM-117 (October 2018)
- Class C subject exhibited extreme aggression after 98 hours of reanimation
- Demonstrated hunting behavior and attempted consumption of organic material
- Showed unusual resistance to standard termination protocols
- Required specialized containment materials beyond initial preparation
- Led to development of enhanced physical restraint systems
- Established first zombie-specific containment protocols

### RM-117 Containment Breach
- Subject escaped primary containment during night shift (October 17, 2018)
- Demonstrated unexpected strength and environmental manipulation
- Injured two security personnel during attempted recapture
- Required specialized response team with enhanced equipment
- Contained after 47 minutes within secured laboratory area
- Provided valuable data for restraint system redesign
- Led to implementation of 24/7 enhanced monitoring

### Zombification Process Identification
- Research team identified progression pattern from reanimation to zombification
- Documented consistent timeline across multiple subjects (typically 72-96 hours)
- Observed predictable stages of cognitive deterioration
- Identified measurable biological markers for transition phases
- Established early warning indicators for developing zombie characteristics
- Created progressive containment protocol based on transition indicators
- Developed treatment interventions to potentially slow or halt progression

## The Headquarters Sub-Level Incident (April 2020)

### Breach Circumstances
- Multiple containment failure in Sub-Level 3 laboratories (April 12, 2020)
- Power fluctuation disabled primary electromagnetic locks during system test
- Backup systems failed to engage due to maintenance protocol error
- Five Type 2 zombies escaped containment simultaneously
- Containment team caught in shift transition with reduced personnel
- Emergency lockdown protocols initiated 4 minutes after initial breach
- Sub-Level 3 successfully isolated from main facility

### Incident Progression
- Zombies accessed central corridor connecting laboratory sections
- Research personnel evacuated to secure areas with 91% successful clearance
- Three researchers and two security personnel trapped in Laboratory Section C
- Zombies demonstrated pack behavior not previously documented
- Coordinated response team deployed from upper levels with specialized equipment
- Established multiple containment perimeters within affected area
- Implemented Cerberus Protocol for systematic area reclamation

### Casualties and Outcome
- Two security personnel and one researcher suffered non-fatal injuries
- One researcher exposed to zombie biological material through laceration
- All five zombies successfully neutralized using newly developed protocols
- Affected researcher placed in quarantine for observation
- Enhanced containment systems designed following incident analysis
- Implemented triple-redundant lock systems and power backups
- Developed specialized rapid response team for future containment breaches

### Technical Response
- Complete redesign of containment systems with enhanced redundancy
- Implementation of power fluctuation isolation technology
- Development of rapid-response protocols for security personnel
- Creation of specialized containment suits for response teams
- Enhanced monitoring system with AI pattern recognition
- Implementation of automated emergency responses
- Development of comprehensive incident response framework

## The Moore Building Event (November 2021)

### Laboratory Transport Failure
- Incident began during routine subject transport between facilities (November 8, 2021)
- Specialized containment vehicle collision on private access road
- Driver incapacitated by mechanical failure causing safety restraint deployment
- Type 3 zombie (Subject ZF-231) freed from damaged containment unit
- Security escort initially unaware of containment breach
- Subject escaped crash scene and entered nearby Moore Building (administrative offices)
- Building contained 73 employees unaware of the situation

### Corporate Response
- Code Red emergency declared at 15:42 hours
- Building immediately locked down with all exits secured
- Specialized Cerberus containment team deployed within 7 minutes
- Executive floor evacuation prioritized through secure elevator system
- Systematic floor-by-floor search protocol implemented
- Building-wide announcement claiming "security drill in progress"
- Deployment of specialized non-lethal containment weapons

### Incident Escalation
- Subject demonstrated unprecedented environmental adaptation
- Used ventilation system to evade initial search pattern
- Infiltrated executive conference room during active meeting
- Attacked three senior executives including VP of Operations
- Attempted to access secure server room through maintenance corridors
- Displayed apparent targeted movement patterns inconsistent with Type 3 profile
- Led pursuit team through complex evasion sequence

### Containment Achievement
- Subject successfully contained after 3 hours and 17 minutes
- Specialized nets and paralytic compounds used for capture
- One Cerberus team member seriously injured during final confrontation
- Subject secured in enhanced portable containment unit
- Three exposed executives placed in immediate quarantine
- Building security footage wiped and replaced with fabricated content
- All Moore Building employees administered "routine vaccinations"

### Media Suppression Operation
- Incident officially classified as "industrial accident with hazardous materials"
- Specialized PR team deployed with prepared cover story
- Building quarantine explained as "airborne chemical contamination"
- Medical response team portrayed as routine hazmat procedure
- Strategic information release through controlled media channels
- Regulatory compliance documentation falsified for official record
- Legal team prepared with employee non-disclosure enforcement

## The Arctic Facility Breach (November 2022)

### Cold-Environment Adaptation
- First observed in Subject ZB-104 during low-temperature testing
- Subject demonstrated unexpected cold-adaptation capabilities
- Maintained functionality in sub-zero temperatures without degradation
- Showed enhanced tissue preservation in extreme cold
- Developed structural changes to cell membranes in freezing conditions
- Created entirely new threat category for containment considerations
- Led to designation of "Type 4 - Arctic Variant" classification

### Primary Containment Failure
- Breach occurred during facility-wide power fluctuation
- Backup systems engaged but Subject ZB-104 already free of restraints
- Automated containment doors compromised during system reset
- Subject successfully navigated to facility perimeter
- Escaped into Arctic environment through emergency access point
- Exterior conditions (-40°F) expected to neutralize subject
- Standard recovery protocols initiated

### Unexpected Survival
- Subject survived extreme conditions for 72 hours before recovery
- Demonstrated remarkable adaptation to Arctic environment
- Maintained locomotion and hunting behaviors in sub-zero conditions
- Traveled 28 miles from facility during freedom period
- Successfully hunted local wildlife showing targeting preference for warm-blooded prey
- Developed ice crystallization in tissue without functional impairment
- Created significant new understanding of zombie environmental adaptability

### Scientific Implications
- Revolutionized understanding of zombie environmental adaptability
- Established "environmental specialization hypothesis" for reanimated tissue
- Created new research focus on environmental factors in zombification
- Led to development of specialized containment protocols for variant types
- Initiated "Adaptive Response Research Program" within the ReAnimus Initiative
- Enhanced focus on environmental factors in containment design
- Resulted in facility-wide security protocol redesign

## Pneumonia Pete's Immunity Discovery (March 2020)

### Accidental Exposure Event
- Occurred during routine consultation visit to ReAnimus laboratories
- Containment seal integrity compromised during observation session
- Pete exposed to active zombie biological material through minor skin laceration
- Immediate decontamination protocols implemented according to standard procedure
- Pete placed in medical isolation for observation and treatment
- Expected zombification timeline established based on previous cases
- Emergency succession planning initiated as precautionary measure

### Unexpected Immunity Observation
- No infection progression observed after 72-hour threshold
- Blood samples revealed no markers of zombification process

- Detailed analysis revealed unique antibody configuration
- Correlation identified with Pete's pneumonia adaptation mechanisms
- Discovery of physiological resistance to neural degradation pathways
- Complete immunity confirmed after 168-hour observation period

### Scientific Breakthrough
- Comprehensive analysis of Pete's blood composition initiated
- Identified specialized antibody production preventing zombification
- Discovered correlation between pneumonia adaptations and zombie resistance
- Established connection to Pete's unique medical history
- Created foundation for synthetic immunity research
- Development of temporary immunity transfer protocol from Pete's blood
- Initiated "Immunity Genesis" program as major research focus

### Immunity Development Program
- Extraction of key compounds from Pete's blood samples
- Creation of first-generation immunity serum with 24-hour effectiveness
- Implementation of regular blood donation protocol for research supply
- Development of synthetic replication for core immunity components
- Creation of enhanced compounds based on Pete's physiology
