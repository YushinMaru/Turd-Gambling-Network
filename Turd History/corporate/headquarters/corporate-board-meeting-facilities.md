## Context
This document details the various facilities used by The Board for their meetings and operations. Due to the secretive nature of The Board, information about these facilities is limited and often contradictory. This documentation compiles verified information alongside persistent reports from reliable sources to provide the most comprehensive available overview of The Board's meeting environments.

## Primary Meeting Chamber: "The Void"

### Physical Characteristics
The Board's primary meeting facility is located deep beneath the Turd Bird headquarters campus in a specially constructed chamber known colloquially as "The Void."

- **Location:** Subterranean level, approximately 200 feet below ground level
- **Access:** Dedicated elevator system with no external controls or indicators
- **Dimensions:** Perfectly circular chamber, 50 feet in diameter, 20 feet ceiling height
- **Construction:** Walls composed of specialized sound-absorbing material that appears to absorb light at certain frequencies
- **Air Supply:** Completely independent ventilation system with triple redundancy
- **Power:** Isolated power source with no connection to main facility grid
- **Communications:** Complete signal isolation from outside world
- **Temperature:** Maintained precisely at 19.85°C (reference to company founding year 1985)
- **Lighting:** Illumination from untraceable sources with no visible fixtures
- **Acoustic Properties:** Perfect sound dampening with specialized white noise generation

### Furniture and Equipment
The chamber contains minimal furnishings with highly specialized design:

- **Seating Arrangement:** 13 identical seats arranged in perfect circle
  - Chairs constructed of unknown dark material resembling obsidian
  - Each seat positioned equidistant from others and center point
  - No identifying marks or distinctions between positions
  - Seats reportedly adjust automatically to occupant's physical dimensions
  - No armrests, forcing neutral posture during deliberations

- **Central Element:** Unmarked circular platform at exact center
  - Perfectly flat circular surface approximately 10 feet in diameter
  - Material resembles polished stone but exhibits unusual properties
  - Surface can transform into interactive display when activated
  - Contains concealed projection and holographic capabilities
  - Rumored to function as "reality sampler" for examining multiple decision outcomes

- **Technological Elements:** No visible technology or controls
  - No screens, keyboards, or conventional interfaces visible
  - Reporting indicates thought-responsive systems may be present
  - Communication systems activated through specific coded gestures
  - Environmental controls responsive to Board consensus rather than individual commands
  - Reality manipulation capabilities rumored but unconfirmed

- **Access Control:**
  - Location known only to Board members and select security personnel
  - Transit system reconfigures after each use, preventing path tracking
  - Biometric verification includes both standard and "non-standard" metrics
  - Identity confirmation protocols reportedly include consciousness verification
  - "Quantum signature authentication" referenced in security documentation

- **Surveillance Countermeasures:**
  - Complete electromagnetic isolation preventing all signal transmission
  - Temporal disruption field preventing recording equipment functioning
  - Cognitive interference systems affecting unauthorized observers
  - Reality anchors preventing dimensional observation techniques
  - Memory verification protocols conducted after all meetings

- **Emergency Systems:**
  - Independent life support with 30-day sustainability
  - Multiple evacuation routes reconfigured after each meeting
  - "Reality reset" capability mentioned in emergency protocols
  - Complete chamber neutralization option (nature unspecified)
  - Specialized countermeasures for Fred Turd infiltration attempts

## Meeting Protocols and Rotation System

### Standard Meeting Schedule
Board meetings follow a complex scheduling system:

- **Regular Sessions:**
  - Monthly primary meetings following lunar cycle
  - Quarterly extended sessions during solstices and equinoxes
  - Annual strategic planning during company founding anniversary
  - Emergency sessions called through specialized notification system

- **Location Rotation System:**
  - Primary location (The Void) used during new moon
  - First quarter moon meetings at terrestrial facilities (Montana/North Dakota)
  - Full moon sessions at underwater facility
  - Last quarter sessions using mobile platform
  - Solstice and equinox meetings in quantum pocket dimension
  - Anniversary meeting at original lighthouse

- **Attendance Requirements:**
  - Quorum definitions changing based on meeting type and lunar phase
  - Specific Board members required for certain decision types
  - Proxy attendance permitted only through specialized clone technology
  - Remote participation prohibited regardless of circumstances
  - Consecutive absence protocols triggering "contingency measures"

### Security Protocols
Extensive measures protect meeting integrity:

- **Information Containment:**
  - No written records permitted except in specialized formats
  - Memory compartmentalization procedures for all participants
  - Temporal isolation preventing information transfer across timelines
  - Reality anchoring preventing "decision leakage" into alternates
  - Specialized mnemonic techniques for decision preservation

- **Physical Security:**
  - Location information distributed through fragmented delivery
  - Transit conducted through multiple redirections and false destinations
  - Temporal displacement during transit preventing tracking
  - Reality camouflage techniques obscuring actual movements
  - Specialized countermeasures against Fred's observation techniques

- **Integrity Verification:**
  - Identity confirmation through conventional and "alternative" means
  - Consciousness verification ensuring mental continuity
  - Temporal alignment checking preventing "replacement from adjacents"
  - Decision consistency metrics tracking member behavior patterns
  - Reality baseline verification before critical decisions

### The Thursday Protocol
A distinctive procedural element in Board operations:

- Thursdays are never acknowledged during Board proceedings
- Meetings extending across Thursday boundaries implement "day displacement"
- Documentation skips from Wednesday to Friday in all records
- Thursday-specific vocabulary prohibited during proceedings
- Alternative day designations substituted when reference necessary
- Rumored connection to "The Thursday Incident" between Fred and Larry

## Cultural Impact

The Board's meeting facilities have generated significant folklore within Turd Bird Industries:

- **"The Summoning":** Term for receiving Board meeting notification

- **"The 13th Chair":** Superstition regarding empty seat occasionally reported
- **"Lighthouse Effect":** Temporary precognition allegedly following original lighthouse meetings
- **"Thursday Blindness":** Inability to recognize or acknowledge Thursdays after Board exposure
- **"Breathing The Void":** Expression for experiencing altered consciousness during meetings
- **"Decision Echo":** Reported phenomenon where major decisions create physical sensations
