## Context
This document details the specialized architectural and logistical systems enabling Frederick "Fred" Werner Turd's infamous practice of riding his armored horse, Redundancy, throughout Turd Bird Industries headquarters. What began as an eccentric executive habit has evolved into a fully integrated aspect of corporate architecture, security protocols, and organizational culture.
