# Event Visualization Prompt Template
**Edition #1.0.0 | Created: (NEUR-ARC-001) | Last Modified: (NEUR-ARC-001)**

Use this template for creating consistent event visualization prompts across the Turd Bird Universe.

## Template Format

```
# [EVENT NAME] - [VISUALIZATION TYPE]
**Style:** Hyperrealistic cinematic scene with dramatic tension, emotional depth, character accuracy, environmental detail, and narrative clarity

**Focus:** [What this specific visualization emphasizes about the event]

**Usage Context:** [Where/how this visualization would be used in documentation]

## Core Prompt
Hyperrealistic cinematic scene depicting [EVENT NAME], [brief event description]. [Setting details]. [Key characters present]. [Critical action moment]. [Emotional atmosphere]. [Environmental response]. [Lighting emphasizing mood]. 8K resolution, photorealistic, cinematic composition, [specific perspective].

## Element Breakdown
- **Setting:** [Detailed description of location where event occurs]
- **Characters:** [Key participants with accurate physical and emotional states]
- **Action:** [The pivotal moment or interaction being depicted]
- **Tension:** [Visual elements that convey the event's significance]
- **Aftermath:** [Visible consequences or reactions to the central action]
- **Symbolism:** [Visual elements that reinforce thematic aspects]
- **Lighting:** [Description of light source, quality, color temperature, and mood]
- **Perspective:** [Viewpoint, distance, angle best capturing the moment]

## Variations

## References

## Example Implementation

```
# The Thursday Incident - Confrontation Moment
**Style:** Hyperrealistic cinematic scene with dramatic tension, emotional depth, character accuracy, environmental detail, and narrative clarity

**Focus:** The pivotal confrontation between Fred Turd and Larry Bird that transformed their relationship from productive antagonism to open conflict

**Usage Context:** Primary event visualization for "The Thursday Incident" documentation

## Core Prompt
Hyperrealistic cinematic scene depicting "The Thursday Incident," the catastrophic board meeting where Fred Turd and Larry Bird's working relationship shattered. Turd Bird Industries executive boardroom with floor-to-ceiling windows overlooking industrial campus. Fred Turd standing aggressively at head of table with ReAnimus Initiative documents projected behind him, Larry Bird ("Francis") standing in opposition with visible emotional distress, Board members in shadow watching, Pneumonia Pete visibly concerned. Moment when Fred called Larry "Francis Underpantsis" during critical presentation. Atmosphere of professional apocalypse with papers scattered. Dramatic lighting from boardroom fixtures creating sharp dividing shadows across table. 8K resolution, photorealistic, cinematic composition, wide shot capturing entire confrontation tableau.

## Element Breakdown
- **Setting:** Turd Bird Industries executive boardroom, 30th floor of headquarters, minimalist luxury with views of campus through floor-to-ceiling windows, large table with ReAnimus Initiative documents and proposals scattered across surface, presentation screens showing controversial research images, subtle security measures visible in architecture
- **Characters:** Fred Turd (58) in bespoke suit with subtle armor modifications, expression combining mockery and rage, standing aggressively at head of table; Larry Bird (62) in immaculate charcoal suit with trace of mustard on pants, face showing rare emotional breakdown with tears forming, standing in opposition; The Board members seated in shadows with expressions of concern and calculation; Pneumonia Pete standing near wall with medical clipboard, expression showing strategic assessment of situation
- **Action:** The exact moment Fred publicly used the "Francis Underpantsis" trigger during a critical ReAnimus Initiative presentation, gesture indicating deliberate psychological attack, Larry in rare moment of composure break, documents scattered showing confrontation has become physical
- **Tension:** Physical postures showing imminent potential for violence, scattered papers and knocked-over chair indicating escalation has already begun, security personnel visible at edges beginning to move toward situation, digital displays showing ReAnimus research with disturbing imagery adding contextual tension
- **Aftermath:** Visible board member reactions indicating critical corporate decision point, security protocols visibly activating, campus visible through windows showing unusual activity pattern suggesting emergency response beginning, communication devices lighting up around room
- **Symbolism:** Lighting creating sharp division across boardroom table symbolizing corporate schism, ReAnimus imagery showing human/zombie research juxtaposed with conflict, subtle reflection of Fred's multiple shadows suggesting clone technology aspect, Larry's perfectly straight tie contrasting with emotional disarray
- **Lighting:** Dramatic overhead boardroom lighting creating sharp corporate shadows, late afternoon sunlight streaming through windows creating long shadows, emergency system lights beginning to activate with subtle red warning hue, presentation screens casting eerie glow on faces
- **Perspective:** Wide cinematic shot capturing entire boardroom tableau while focusing on Fred-Larry confrontation, angle emphasizing power dynamics between figures, depth showing Board members in background as judges of the conflict

## Variations
1. Close-up on Larry's face showing rare emotional break moment with tear visible
2. Perspective from behind The Board showing both men from evaluation perspective
3. Aftermath moment showing security personnel restraining Fred as Larry exits boardroom
4. Symbolic composition emphasizing ReAnimus presentation images juxtaposed with human conflict
