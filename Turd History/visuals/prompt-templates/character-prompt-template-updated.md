# Character Visualization Prompt Template (Texas Edition)
**Edition #1.1.0 | Created: (NEUR-ARC-001) | Last Modified: (THEME-001)**

> Previous: Edition #1.0.0

Use this template for creating consistent character visualization prompts across the Turd Bird Universe, incorporating the Texas theme elements that distinguish characters with authentic Texas backgrounds from those with affected Texas personas.

## Template Format

```
# [CHARACTER NAME] - [VISUALIZATION TYPE]
**Style:** Hyperrealistic digital art with subtle surrealist elements, cinematic lighting, strong shadows, high detail, corporate aesthetic with elements of chaos where appropriate, Texas motifs where relevant

**Focus:** [What this specific visualization emphasizes about the character]

**Usage Context:** [Where/how this visualization would be used in documentation]

**Texas Element:** [Authentic regional background OR Affected/theatrical Texas persona]

## Core Prompt
Hyperrealistic portrait of [CHARACTER NAME], [brief character description]. [Physical appearance details]. [Texas-specific clothing/accessories]. [Environmental elements]. [Expression/pose]. [Lighting/mood]. 8K resolution, photorealistic, cinematic lighting, [specific perspective].

## Element Breakdown
- **Subject:** [Detailed description of character's physical appearance, including distinctive features, build, age indicators]
- **Texas Authenticity:** [For authentic characters: genuine regional elements | For affected characters: theatrical/exaggerated Texas elements]
- **Clothing:** [Detailed description of outfit, including Texas/Western elements, textures, colors, distinctive elements]
- **Accessories:** [Key Texas-themed items character has or wears that define their look]
- **Environment:** [Background/setting elements that provide context, potentially incorporating Western/Texas motifs]
- **Pose/Action:** [How the character is positioned or what they're doing, possibly including horseback elements where appropriate]
- **Expression:** [Facial expression and the emotion it conveys]
- **Lighting:** [Description of light source, quality, color temperature, and mood]
- **Perspective:** [Camera angle, framing, distance]

## Variations
1. [Alternative angle/perspective]
2. [Alternative lighting condition]
3. [Alternative emotional state]
4. [Alternative environmental context with Texas theme]

## References

```

## Example Implementation: Authentic Texas Background

```
# Eleanor "Ellie" Blande - Primary Portrait
**Style:** Hyperrealistic digital art with subtle surrealist elements, cinematic lighting, strong shadows, high detail, corporate aesthetic with authentic Texas elements

**Focus:** Core personality traits and physical presence as Fred's organizational anchor and genuine Texas counterpoint

**Usage Context:** Primary character reference visualization for overview documentation

**Texas Element:** Authentic regional background with genuine Texas upbringing

## Core Prompt
Hyperrealistic portrait of Eleanor "Ellie" Blande, Fred Turd's 19-year-old personal assistant and organizational anchor. Slender athletic build with blonde hair in a practical ponytail, clear blue observant eyes, and a natural tan from authentic Texas outdoor upbringing. Wearing professional business attire with subtle Western influences and practical boots suitable for both office environments and sudden horse-related duties. Standing confidently in a corporate setting with subtle ranch elements in background. Expression combines efficiency, alertness, and quiet competence. Natural morning light with warm tones. 8K resolution, photorealistic, cinematic lighting, three-quarter view.

## Element Breakdown
- **Subject:** 19-year-old female, 5'4", slender athletic build, blonde hair in practical ponytail, clear blue observant eyes with rapid scanning pattern, natural tan, small scar through right eyebrow from youth horseback incident
- **Texas Authenticity:** Genuine regional characteristics without exaggeration, authentic posture and bearing developed through practical ranch work, natural comfort with Western elements rather than affected display
- **Clothing:** Professional business attire with subtle Western influences in neutral tones with occasional orange accent, practical yet stylish, designed for movement and horseback mobility, contains hidden tactical pockets for "Fred emergency supplies"
- **Accessories:** Stylish but functional communication device disguised as fashion item, custom Turd Bird tablet with Fred tracking software, specialized satchel containing Fred essentials and horse treats
- **Environment:** Professional corporate setting with subtle ranch elements visible (perhaps small family ranch photo, authentic Texas memorabilia), suggestion of equestrian equipment nearby
- **Pose/Action:** Standing confidently with perfect posture, alert stance suggesting perpetual readiness, positioning that indicates awareness of surroundings and exit points
- **Expression:** Efficient alertness with quiet competence, eyes showing observant intelligence and practical problem-solving, subtle hint of amusement at corporate absurdity
- **Lighting:** Natural morning light with warm tones, suggesting Texas sunrise quality, practical rather than dramatic illumination
- **Perspective:** Three-quarter view showing face and posture, straight-on angle reflecting directness and honesty

## Variations
1. Same character but helping Fred mount his horse in a corporate boardroom setting
2. Same character but organizing Fred's conspiracy files with tactical efficiency
3. Same character but demonstrating authentic Texas horsemanship in contrast to Fred's affected style
4. Same character but calmly managing a crisis Fred created while maintaining perfect composure

## Example Implementation: Affected Texas Persona

```
# Frederick "Fred" Werner Turd - Primary Portrait
**Style:** Hyperrealistic digital art with subtle surrealist elements, cinematic lighting, strong shadows, high detail, corporate aesthetic with exaggerated Texas elements

**Focus:** Core personality traits and physical presence as the chaotic genius founder with affected Texas persona

**Usage Context:** Primary character reference visualization for overview documentation

**Texas Element:** Exaggerated/theatrical Texas persona despite New Jersey origins

## Core Prompt
Hyperrealistic portrait of Frederick "Fred" Turd, chaotic business magnate and founder of Turd Bird Industries. 58-year-old portly man with disproportionate upper body strength, manicured silver-streaked hair slicked back, heterochromatic eyes (one green, one amber) with independently dilating pupils. Wearing a bespoke Texas-style light brown suit with ostentatious Western details, custom belt buckle, and subtle bolo tie despite New Jersey origins. Mounted on his armored horse "Redundancy" in his executive office with bizarre technological prototypes in background. Expression combines arrogance, amusement, and calculating intelligence. Dramatic side lighting creating sharp contrast. 8K resolution, photorealistic, cinematic lighting, three-quarter view.

## Element Breakdown
- **Subject:** 58-year-old stocky male, 5'10", portly with disproportionate upper body strength, manicured silver-streaked hair slicked back, heterochromatic eyes (green/amber) with independently dilating pupils, subtly scarred face with tan lines, goatee, perpetual smirk
- **Texas Affectation:** Deliberately exaggerated Texas persona despite New Jersey origins, theatrical Western mannerisms, affected drawl suggested through posture and expression, unnaturally even tan with visible tan lines from proprietary tanning process
- **Clothing:** Bespoke Texas-style light brown suit with ostentatious Western details and oversized proportions, exaggerated design elements including subtle bolo tie and custom belt buckle, high-quality materials with unconventional details suggesting both wealth and eccentricity
- **Accessories:** Modified Rolex with Texas state seal, zombie-tooth cufflinks, gas mask pocket square, subtle armor modifications to suit, oversized gold belt buckle featuring concealed defensive capabilities, custom ostrich leather boots with hidden compartments
- **Environment:** Executive office with floor-to-ceiling windows showing industrial campus, bizarre prototypes and experimental devices scattered throughout, mixture of luxury and mad scientist aesthetic with theatrical Western elements
- **Pose/Action:** Mounted on armored horse "Redundancy" in awkward but confident seated position, exaggerated Western riding posture despite limited actual equestrian skill, one hand holding reins, other gesturing as if explaining a concept
- **Expression:** Self-assured smirk with slight asymmetry, eyes showing different emotions (calculation and amusement), eyebrow slightly raised, performative confidence masking paranoia
- **Lighting:** Dramatic side lighting creating strong shadow contrast, emphasizing facial asymmetry, warm golden hour light filtering through windows with cool blue accent lights from technology
- **Perspective:** Three-quarter view showing face and mounted posture, slight low angle suggesting power position, framing that emphasizes the absurdity of being mounted on horseback indoors

## Variations
1. Same pose but in boardroom setting with executives looking concerned in background
2. Same character but riding through office hallway with exaggerated Western riding posture
3. Same character but at night with experimental technology casting eerie glow
4. Same character but with multiple Fred clones visible in various states of completion in background, all wearing matching Texas-style suits
