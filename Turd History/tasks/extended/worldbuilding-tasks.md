## HIGH Priority Worldbuilding Tasks

### [WRLD-101] - Design The Board's Meeting Protocols and Locations
**Priority:** HIGH
**Category:** WORLDBUILDING
**Status:** COMPLETED+VERIFIED
**Related Characters:** The Board, Fred Turd
**Timeline Position:** 1985-Present

**Description:**
Create detailed documentation of The Board's bizarre meeting protocols, rituals, and locations, including abandoned missile silos, underwater facilities, quantum-unstable pocket dimensions, the voting protocols that change based on lunar cycles, and the ceremonial behaviors that govern Board operations.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- Document at least five distinct meeting locations with descriptions
- Create detailed procedural documentation for Board meetings
- Explain the lunar-cycle-based voting protocols
- Document the ban on direct eye contact and communication via reflective surfaces
- Detail the "Quarterly Shuffle" interpretive corporate dance
- Explain the "Quantum Decision Matrix" dartboard decision-making process
- Document the peculiar traditions like the annual "Redundancy Ritual"
- Explain the "Thursday Protocol" that prohibits acknowledging Thursdays

**Dependencies:**
- Beneficial to complete after [RELP-101]

**Notes:**
These absurdist corporate governance mechanisms provide insight into The Board's mysterious nature and explain some of Turdbird's more inexplicable decision-making.

**Completion Details:**
Completed on 2025-05-07. See [/tasks/completed-tasks.md § WRLD-101] for full completion details and verification notes.
