## Potential Inconsistencies Resolved

After thorough analysis, several potential minor inconsistencies were identified and resolved:

### 1. Construction Timeline Clarification
While character-fred-turd-development-arc.md mentions "Construction of fortified personal residence" during 2010-2015, and relationship-fred-mansion-pete.md states the moat was installed in December 2018, these are not contradictory. The mansion was built during 2010-2015, while the specific Pete-targeting defenses (particularly the freezing moat) were added later as the relationship deteriorated.

### 2. Relationship Timeline Integration
The timing of the Coffee Poisoning Incident (January 2020) occurs after the installation of the freezing moat (December 2018), suggesting Fred's preparations against Pete preceded the formal breakdown of their relationship. This timeline progression is consistent when viewing the overall arc of their relationship deterioration.

### 3. Temperature Variance Analysis
All specific mentions of the moat temperature consistently cite exactly -12.3°C. Monitoring alerts are set for "any variance above -12.0°C" which is consistent with maintaining the critical -12.3°C temperature.

### 4. Security Staff Count Verification
The security staff is consistently documented as 24 personnel across all references.
